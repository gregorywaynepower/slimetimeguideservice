Who We Are

Here at Slime Time we offer more, dollar for dollar than any other guide service On the lakes we target and species we specialize in. We offer trips 12 months out of the year, 7 days a week for multiple species and bodies of water. We back all that up with a No Catch No Pay Policy. By us guarenteeing your success if the species of your choice does not cooperate or you are not fully satisfied at the end of the trip, you don't pay a dime!

Filleting Services

Fillet and bagging of your entire day's catch at the end of your trip at no extra cost! We want to remove the thought and worry of spending money on booking a trip and coming home empty-handed. The optional free filleting and packaging of your day's catch is another benefit of choosing us. We offer this for our customers, especially those traveling long distances to fish with us and arriving with smaller vehicles that do not allow for larger coolers. We want you to head home after your day's trip without the mess and hassle of cleaning a full day's catch of fish when you get home. You can have four times the fish in a cooler half the size!

The Fish You Can Catch!

  • Catfish

    We catch Channel, Blue, and Flathead Catfish. Great fish to target all year. Not uncommon to catch multiple of these each day, ranging from 20 to 50 pounds each.

  • Black Crappie

    A great fish to target all year long. We mainly target these fish on Falls Lake and Kerr Lake. We have many trips throughout the year where we will boat 100 to 200+ fish a day!

  • Largemouth Bass

    America's #1 sport fish. We typically only use live bait on our largemouth trips, which drastically increases your chances that fish of a lifetime. Trips are based on Falls Lake due to large number of trophy-sized fish.

  • Striped Bass

    One of the hardest fighting fish you can catch in a lake, which will give you an excellent meal to take home. More commonly found in Lake Kerr, Lake Gaston, and Roanoke Rapids Reservior. We do not offer No Catch No Pay Policty when targeting these fish alone.

  • White Perch

    A great eating fish that we typically catch in large numbers. Perfect fish for kids and beginners because they are eager to bite the hook. Some rigs we use we can catch multiple fish per rod!

  • White Bass

    Great fighting fish for its size and great table fare. They migrate quite frequently. You will see White Bass schooling with White Perch. We do not offer our No Catch No Pay Policy when targeting this species alone!
